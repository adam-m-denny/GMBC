About Me

I was raised in Massillon, a dirty little steel town in Ohio. The only thing that Massillon was noted for was football. My goal in life was to become a football coach. I was well on my way when my plans were interrupted by an invitation to be all I could be in the Army. It was while I was in the Army that I surrendered to Jesus Christ as my Lord and Savior, and became a born again believer.

After the Army, I attended Baptist Bible College and during my third year married my wife Miriam. I was ordained into the gospel ministry and have been faithful to God's call ministering in various churches. I attended Western Seminary in Portland OR and have been the pastor of Green Mountain Baptist church for going on four years. My wife and I have three grown daughters and have just become grandparents. I enjoy living in the North West and love ministry at Green Mountain.

Church Position Statement

  1. We believe the Bible is the inspired, inerrant Word of God and is authoritative as to what we are to believe and practice.
  2. We believe that there is one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son and the Holy Spirit.
  3. We believe in the Deity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, His sinless life, His death on Calvary for the sins of the world, His ascension to Heaven and His imminent return.
  4. We believe that reconciliation between Holy God and sinful men was made possible, and is only possible through faith in the vicarious death of Jesus Christ on Calvary.
  5. We believe that the Holy Spirit is the third person of the trinity, who works to convict of sin, to glorify Christ, to illumine Scripture, and to bear fruit in the life of believers.
  6. We believe in the existence of Satan, who opposes and mimics the work of God in this world, working to blind people to their spiritual need.
  7. We believe Christ will call the saints out of this world (the Rapture) and will return seven years later with the saints to establish His Kingdom on the earth.
  8. We believe in the separation of Church and State.
  9. We believe Christ gave the church two ordinances to observe, both of which remind us of his sacrificial death for the salvation of men: baptism and communion.
  10. We believe in the autonomy (independence & self rule) of the local church
  11. We believe that believers are to be active in Evangelistic outreach at home and abroad.

The Bible - God's Word

The Bible contains: the Mind of God, the state of Man, the Way of Salvation, the doom of sinners, and the happiness of believers. Its doctrines are Holy, its precepts are binding, its histories are true, and its decisions are immutable. Read it to be wise, believe it to be safe, and practice it to be Holy.

It contains light to direct you, food to support you and comfort to cheer you. It is the traveler's map, the pilgrim's staff, the pilot's compass, the soldier's sword, and the Christian's charter.
